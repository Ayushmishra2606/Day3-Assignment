Storyline

Syed Bashir, a redheaded Pakistani, meets Carlos Pedro Panther, a dance-loving don, in Muscat to retrieve a reel containing secret information about Indian officials. The reel has been circulating globally. Carlos's associate, Jagawar, encounters Laila in India, who has the reel after killing her husband. Laila negotiates with Carlos for the reel in exchange for an undisclosed item.
